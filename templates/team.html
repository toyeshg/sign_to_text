About

Welcome to our innovative platform designed to bridge communication barriers between the deaf and hearing communities through cutting-edge technology. Our Sign Language to Text Converter and Vice Versa is a pioneering tool crafted with the mission to facilitate seamless communication, inclusivity, and understanding.

Key Features

  • Real-Time Conversion: Our platform offers seamless conversion between sign language and text in real-time, enabling smooth and instant communication. User-Friendly Interface: Designed with usability in mind, our interface is intuitive and easy to navigate, ensuring a seamless user experience for both deaf and hearing individuals.
  • Accessibility: We are committed to accessibility for all. Our platform adheres to the highest standards of accessibility, ensuring that individuals with diverse needs can use our tool with ease.

How It Works

Using advanced computer vision and natural language processing algorithms, our platform analyzes sign language gestures and converts them into text in real-time. Similarly, it interprets text input and generates corresponding sign language animations or displays. This seamless bidirectional conversion facilitates smooth communication between deaf and hearing individuals, transcending linguistic barriers.
